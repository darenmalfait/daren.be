I designed a modern, responsive website for By Hans Depla to showcase their cars and services effectively. The previous site lacked maintainability and adaptability to different devices, which hindered its usability. The new website solves these issues, ensuring seamless navigation across all platforms.

The key focus was to make it easy for Hans to manage the inventory of cars. With an intuitive interface, adding, editing, and removing cars is now straightforward, saving time and effort. Each car listing is visually appealing and informative, enhancing the browsing experience for customers.

Additionally, the site highlights their services like repairs, rentals, and custom welding. The clean layout and responsive design ensure visitors find information quickly. This modern approach positions By Hans Depla as a professional, customer-focused business.
