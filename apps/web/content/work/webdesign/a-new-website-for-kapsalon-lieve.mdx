The website for Kapsalon Lieve prioritizes simplicity and essential information. We created a clean, easy-to-navigate design where visitors can quickly find services and contact details. The minimalistic approach ensures an efficient user experience that makes it easy for potential clients to reach out.

The website structure includes just the key elements needed - sections for salon services and contact information. A straightforward contact form allows customers to easily inquire about services or schedule appointments, serving the site's core purpose without added complexity.

The responsive design ensures smooth functionality across all devices, with a mobile-optimized layout for easy access to contact details on the go. Fast loading times and adaptive design provide a seamless experience whether browsing on desktop or smartphone.
